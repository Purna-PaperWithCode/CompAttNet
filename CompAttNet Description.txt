# CompAttNet
## Composition Attention Network for Materials Property Prediction

### Overview

CompAttNet is a lightweight composition-attention deep learning framework developed for predicting thermodynamic and electronic properties of inorganic materials directly from their chemical compositions. The framework employs a multi-head attention mechanism to learn element-level interactions from stoichiometric information without requiring explicit crystallographic descriptors.

The proposed framework is designed for efficient composition-driven materials screening and supports scalable prediction of multiple scalar material properties using a unified neural architecture.

---

## Scientific Motivation

Accurate prediction of material properties is fundamental to accelerating computational materials discovery. Although graph neural networks and other structure-aware models have achieved excellent predictive performance, they rely on crystal structural information that is often unavailable during the early stages of material design.

CompAttNet addresses this limitation by learning composition–property relationships directly from chemical formulas, enabling efficient prediction using only stoichiometric information.

---

## Key Features

• Lightweight multi-head attention architecture
